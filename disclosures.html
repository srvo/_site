Forward-Looking Statements

This website may contain forward-looking statements regarding our expectations about future market conditions, investment performance, or business operations. These statements are based on current expectations and assumptions that may prove to be incorrect. Actual results may differ materially from those expressed or implied in forward-looking statements.

Risk Disclosures

Investment Risks

  • Market Risk: All securities are subject to market volatility and may lose value
  • ESG Risk: ESG screening may limit investment opportunities and affect performance
  • Concentration Risk: Focused portfolios may be more volatile than diversified ones
  • Liquidity Risk: Some investments may be difficult to sell during market stress
  • Regulatory Risk: Changes in laws and regulations may impact investment performance

ESG-Specific Risks

  • Limited Universe: ESG screening reduces the investable universe, which may impact diversification
  • Data Quality: ESG data may be incomplete, inaccurate, or subject to interpretation
  • Evolving Standards: ESG criteria and methodologies continue to evolve and may change over time
  • Performance Impact: ESG constraints may result in performance that differs from broader market indices
