# ECIC Brand Style Guide

This page demonstrates all the visual elements, typography, and styling used throughout the Ethical Capital website.

## Color Palette

Our professional purple and teal palette reflects our commitment to investment excellence and innovation.

::: {.color-palette}

**Primary Colors**
- **Purple Primary** `#581c87` - Primary brand color (professional excellence)
- **Purple Hover** `#6b46c1` - Secondary color (stability, trust)

**Accent Colors**  
- **Teal Accent** `#14b8a6` - Growth and innovation
- **Light Teal** `#2dd4bf` - Harmony and progress
- **Amber Warning** `#f59e0b` - Caution and balance
- **Red Danger** `#ef4444` - Risk and attention

**Supporting Colors**
- **Text Primary** `#111827` - Main text
- **Text Secondary** `#4b5563` - Secondary text
- **Background** `#ffffff` - Page background
- **Surface** `#f9fafb` - Card backgrounds
- **Border** `#e5e7eb` - Subtle borders

:::

## Branding Elements

### Logo Usage
- **Primary Logo**: ECIC logo in deep forest green
- **Logo Placement**: Top navigation, footer, and key brand moments
- **Logo Variants**: Standard and alternative versions available

### Brand Voice
- **Professional** yet approachable
- **Evidence-based** and transparent
- **Values-driven** but not preachy
- **Confident** in our methodology

### Visual Hierarchy
- **Clear information architecture** with logical flow
- **Consistent spacing** using 8px grid system
- **Strategic use of color** to guide attention
- **Typography scale** that supports readability

## Special ECIC Classes

**.ecic-logo-text** - Styled text for brand references

**.ecic-tagline** - Subtle styling for taglines and supporting text

## Accessibility

- **High contrast ratios** meet WCAG guidelines
- **Readable font sizes** with generous line spacing
- **Keyboard navigation** support throughout
- **Screen reader** friendly semantic markup

## Usage Guidelines

### Do
- Use colors consistently across all materials
- Maintain generous white space and clean layouts
- Apply professional typography hierarchy
- Include ECIC branding elements appropriately

### Don't  
- Modify brand colors or create new variations
- Use competing fonts or inconsistent typography
- Overcrowd layouts or reduce white space
- Misuse or distort logo elements
